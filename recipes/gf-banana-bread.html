Gluten Free Banana Bread Recipe

This banana bread recipe is quick and easy to make and keeps well!

Ingredients

  • 3 average, very ripe bananas
  • 1.5 cup 1 to 1 Gluten Free Flour mix
  • 6 tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 eggs, scrambled
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • pinch salt
  • 3/4 cup chocolate chips, walnuts, etc

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. In a medium mixing bowl, mash bananas til liquid.
  3. Sprinkle baking soda on mashed bananas, stir in.
  4. Add pinch of salt.
  5. Add butter, sugar, and vanilla. Mix well.
  6. Add eggs, stir in.
  7. Fold in gluten free flour, do not overstir.
  8. Lastly, add in desired mix-ins, chocolate, nuts, etc.
  9. Pour in muffin tray or bread pan, bake until folk inserted in center comes out clean.
  10. Enjoy!
