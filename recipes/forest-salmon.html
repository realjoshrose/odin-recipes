Forest Grilled Salmon Recipe

This salmon dish is easy to prepare and serve, and tastes great with many sides!

Ingredients

  • Salmon Filets
  • Olive Oil - to brush on
  • 1 red apple, diced - firmer varieties better
  • 2/3rds cup Almonds, chopped
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries, chopped
  • 2-3 tablespoons honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Herbs, salt and pepper - as desired

Steps

  1. Heat grill to medium, lined with aluminium foil
  2. Add chopped apple, almonds, and cranberries to small mixing bowl
  3. Mix in honey and cinnamon, ensuring even mix and coating
  4. Set aside this mixture, this will go atop the cooked salmon
  5. Place salmon filets on grill, skin side down on the foil
  6. While cooking, brush with oil and top with herbs or salt and pepper to your taste
  7. Cook until meat is opaque and flakes easily with fork - about 12-15 min usually
  8. Remove salmon from grill, remove skin and foil
  9. Top salmon with fruit/nut mixture and serve
