Gluten Free Chicken Parm Recipe

This recipe takes a little prep work, but it's easy and great for sandwiches or pasta!

Ingredients

  • 2lbs chicken breast, prepped to cutlet size
  • 1 bag gluten free Corn Chex cereal, crushed
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 8oz Parmeasan Cheese
  • marina sauce
  • Canola Oil, to fill frying pan
  • Gluten Free flour
  • Desired spices, to taste

Steps

  1. If not already done, cut chicken into flat cutlets
  2. Add eggs and milk to a bowl, whisk
  3. Add 6oz of cheese to crushed Chex cereal in dish or tray
  4. In a seperate bowl, add flour and desired spice, mix
  5. Heat Canola oil in pan
  6. Take chicken, dip first in flour mix, then egg wash, then cereal
  7. Press cereal into chiken cutlet
  8. Fry chicken cutlet, 5-7 min per side, drain and cool after
  9. Top finished cutlets with marina and cheese
  10. Serve cut with pasta or whole on sandwiches!
